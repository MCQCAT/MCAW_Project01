About Us

Coronary MCQS

It started as a small initiative in the summer semester of 2023, created by a first-year medical student on Telegram . What began as a simple platform for sharing and solving questions among classmates quickly developed into a valuable resource for hundreds of students. This initiative has grown thanks to the active participation of many others, who contribute by writing questions and ensuring our content stays diverse and relevant.

The Present

As we continue to expand, Coronary MCQS remains committed to delivering high-level, reliable multiple-choice questions tailored specifically for medical students. Our focus is shifting towards creating even more specialized content, especially for the clinical years, where the need for practical, scenario-based learning is crucial. With contributions from students, we ensure that our questions reflect real-world medical cases, helping students stay prepared for the challenges ahead. We're excited to keep growing and providing valuable resources for years to come.
