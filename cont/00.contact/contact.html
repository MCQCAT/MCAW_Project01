Coronary MCQS

It started as a small initiative in the summer semester of 2023, created by a first-year medical student on Telegram. What began as a simple platform for sharing and solving questions among classmates quickly developed into a valuable resource for hundreds of students. Originally founded by a single student, this initiative has grown thanks to the active participation of many others, who contribute by writing questions and ensuring our content stays diverse and relevant.
